Product Brief

Product Brief

Before you start creating, take 3 minutes to understand what Immersive Translate does, what makes it unique, and what you must communicate accurately.

Feature Priority Use Case & Key Selling Point
Bilingual Web Translation ★ Must Cover Read English blogs, SaaS dashboards, tech docs, and news side-by-side in your language. Far more readable than standard browser translation — ideal for deep reading and research.
Input Box Translation ★ Must Cover Works in Gmail, Slack, Discord, WhatsApp — type in your language, one click to translate to English. No need to switch tools.
Video Subtitle Translation ★ Must Cover Bilingual subtitles on YouTube tutorials, reviews, and meeting recordings in real time. Supports YouTube Live (streams with native captions only).
BabelDOC (PDF Translation) ★ Doc Creators Translate contracts, technical whitepapers, and complex reports — tables, formulas, and multi-column layouts preserved. Open-source engine, trending on GitHub.
AI Reply ◎ Advanced Summarize long English emails in Gmail / GitHub and draft replies in one click. Adjust tone and send — no context switching needed.
Image / Screenshot Translation ◎ Advanced Translate text in screenshots, memes, and product UI images. Great for sharing English content with local audiences.

Privacy

Privacy Protection

Translation content often involves sensitive information. Immersive Translate places privacy protection at the core of its product design.

🔒
Privacy Mode (OneAIFW)
Local Anonymization · No Plaintext in Cloud
Uses a closed-loop process: Local Detection → Local Anonymization → Cloud Translation → Local Restoration. Sensitive data such as names, addresses, bank card numbers, passwords, and private keys never leave the user's device.
  • Sensitive info replaced with anonymous placeholders; cloud only processes anonymized text
  • Anonymized data stored in browser RAM only, auto-cleared on browser close
  • Open-sourced under MIT license, publicly auditable by the community
🛡️
Zero Data Retention
No Storage · No Training · Fully Encrypted
Zero retention of translation content: no storage of translated text, webpages, or documents. Translation requests and AI results are never used for model training.
  • All translation requests are encrypted in transit
  • Only anonymous usage statistics collected (frequency, device model, IP region)
  • Google Analytics and similar tools can be managed via browser settings
📋
Security Incidents & Privacy Contact
Compliant Response · Replied Within 1 Month
Established Information Security Incident Management Procedures and Emergency Response policies. In the event of a data breach, remediation actions are taken in accordance with applicable laws, and regulators and affected users are notified.
  • Privacy inquiries: support@immersivetranslate.com
  • All queries responded to within one month
